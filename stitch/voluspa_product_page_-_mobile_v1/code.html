Refill Ritual

01

Freeze Remnants

Place your empty glass vessel in the freezer for 2 hours to shrink the remaining wax.

02

Pop & Clean

Gently pop out the old wax with a spoon and wash the vessel with warm, soapy water.

03

Insert Refill

Peel the adhesive cover from the bottom of your new refill and drop it into the dry vessel.

04

Trim & Light

Trim the wick to 1/4 inch and light to enjoy another 60 hours of fragrance.
